## Common Errors and Solutions

### Error: Asset Not Authorized

**Description:** This error occurs when the system detects unauthorized use of an asset.

**Solution:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Purchase">
    Verify that you have purchased the asset from the official Lofi Development store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Installation">
    Ensure that you have installed the asset correctly by following the official installation guide.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Error: File Encryption Failure

**Description:** An error message indicating that a critical file could not be encrypted.

**Solution:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check File Integrity">
    Check that the files are not corrupted or incomplete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Permissions">
    Confirm that you have the necessary permissions to modify the files.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Error: Access Denied

**Description:** Access to the asset is denied due to incorrect user credentials.

**Solution:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Credentials">
    Double-check your login credentials and ensure your account is in good standing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If the problem persists, contact Lofi Development support for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
These solutions aim to address the most common issues experienced with the FiveM asset escrow system and help maintain the integrity and security of your assets. Ensure that you keep your software updated and consult official resources for further troubleshooting steps.
</Info>
