## Installation Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Resource">
    Download the most up to date resource from [GitHub](https://github.com/Lofiith/Lofi_JungleRZ/tree/main).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the Script">
    Drag the "Lofi_JungleRZ" folder into your resources directory.
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Open the "config.lua" file and customize your redzone settings according to your server needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Server">
    Add the following line to your server.cfg file:
    
    ```cfg
    ensure Lofi_JungleRZ
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test & Verify">
    Restart your server and test the redzone functionality. For any support, contact us via [Discord](https://discord.gg/lofidev).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Need help with configuration? Check out our [configuration guide](/essentials/jungle-rz/configuration-guide) or join our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/lofidev) for support.
</Info>
