If you encounter any issues or find that our documentation is missing important information, please don't hesitate to reach out:

- **Discord Support**: [discord.gg/VuMcnbmEby](https://discord.gg/VuMcnbmEby)

## Our Team

JX Scripts is currently a solo development operation focused on delivering quality resources to the FiveM community. As we grow, we're looking to expand our team with talented developers and support staff!

When reaching out for support, please be patient and provide as much detail as possible about your issue, including:
- Your framework (ESX, QBCore, QBX, Standalone)
- FiveM server version
- Any error messages from the console
- Steps to reproduce the issue
